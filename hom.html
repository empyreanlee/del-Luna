Hotel del Luna

A New Dimension of Luxury

Hotel del Luna is located at Portland-Oregon junction. The resort offers a fun filled escape from life hustle and bustle. Boasting over 100 accommodation rooms wrapped in alluring style and modern decor, multiple on-site restaurants serving different cuisines, a lounge bar, conference rooms, a massive pool and extensive gardens with lots of activities for both adults and children.
This is an ideal base for business travellers, family events and holiday seekers. Whether your stay is long or short, there’s a feeling of ease about this environment, with a vibe that's truly one of a kind and prestige that accompanies period décor.
